Acute COPD Exacerbation Prediction Tool (ACCEPT) 

 Please address following questions on your dictation: 

 1-Age (year)*

 2-Is the patient currently a smoker?*

 3- Post-bronchodilator FEV1 (% predicted) * 

 4-St. Georges Respiratory Questionnaire Score (SGRQ) 

 5-If SGRQ is not available, please enter COPD Assessment Test (CAT) Score

 6-Body mass index (BMI) * 

 7-Has the patient received oxygen therapy within the last year? * 

 8-Is the patient currently on statins? * 

 9-Is the patient currently on LAMAs? * 

 10-Is the patient currently on LABAs? * 

 11-Is the patient currently on inhaled corticosteroids? * 

 12-Number of All Exacerbations within the last 12 months * 

 13-Number of Severe Exacerbations within the last 12 months *

        Either SGRQ or CAT score must be entered.

        You can use any order to answer the questions

        You can use the brand or generic names of medications

Please use keyboard or microphone to fill the text then submit the form
